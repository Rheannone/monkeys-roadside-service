Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our mobile diesel service

What areas do you serve?

We primarily service El Paso, Midland, Odessa, and surrounding areas in West Texas. Travel fees may apply for locations outside our primary service area. When you call, we'll confirm if we can reach your location and discuss any applicable fees upfront.

How quickly can you respond?

Response time depends on our current schedule and your location. For emergency roadside situations, we prioritize getting to you as quickly as possible. When you call, we'll discuss current availability and provide an honest estimate of when we can arrive.

What types of diesel vehicles do you work on?

We service diesel trucks (both commercial and personal), diesel pickups (Ford, Dodge, Chevy), RVs with diesel engines, and other diesel-powered vehicles. We work on Cummins, Detroit, Caterpillar, International, and other diesel engine brands. Call to discuss your specific vehicle.

Do you offer 24/7 service?

Yes! We're available 24/7 for emergency calls. For urgent roadside situations, call Juan at 915-407-8114 anytime. Response time depends on our current location and your situation, but we prioritize emergency breakdowns and DOT violations.

How much do you charge?

Pricing varies based on the type of repair, parts needed, and location. We provide a clear quote after diagnosing the issue—before we start any work. You'll know the cost upfront with no surprise charges. Call to discuss your specific situation and get an estimate.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, major credit/debit cards, Zelle, Cash App, and Venmo. Payment is typically due upon completion of service. For commercial customers or larger jobs, we can discuss payment arrangements in advance.

What if you can't fix it on-site?

Most repairs can be completed on-site, but some complex jobs may require parts we don't carry or specialized equipment. If that's the case, we'll provide a detailed diagnosis, explain your options, and help you understand the next steps—whether that's ordering parts for a follow-up visit or recommending a shop if needed.

Do I need to be present during the repair?

You don't need to stay for the entire repair, but we do need to meet you initially to access the vehicle and discuss the issue. Once we've provided a quote and you've approved the work, you can leave if needed. We'll call you when the job is complete.
